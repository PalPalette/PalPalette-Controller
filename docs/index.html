Installation Steps

1

Connect Your Device

Connect your Seeed XIAO ESP32C3 to your computer using a USB cable. Make sure the device is powered on.

2

Click Install Button

Click the "Install PalPalette Controller" button above. A dialog will appear asking you to select your device's serial port.

3

Select Serial Port

Choose your device from the list (usually appears as "USB Serial" or similar). Click "Connect".

4

Wait for Installation

The firmware will be downloaded and flashed to your device. This typically takes 1-2 minutes. Do not disconnect your device during this process.

5

Setup Your Device

After installation, your device will restart and create a WiFi network named "PalPalette-Setup-XXXXXX". Connect to this network to configure your device.
